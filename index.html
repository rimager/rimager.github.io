About

I am a web developer located in Miami, FL.

With a solid background in Computer Science and Engineering, and a love of art and design, I have been captured by the beauty and challenge of Web Design and Development for the last 10+ years. To me, there is nothing more fun and engaging than seeing cold, hard logic and scientific scripts turn into beautiful, usable, creative experiences for everyone to see and engage with.

I am a teacher, but I am also a life-long learner. Technology changes constantly, and no web developer can ever rest on their laurels. Luckily, I love to read and have the patience and persistence to learn about new tools, techniques, and technologies as often as I can.
